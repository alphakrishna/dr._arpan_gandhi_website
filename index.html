I am Dr. Arpan Gandhi, a diagnostic medicine leader from India.

For nearly three decades, I have been deeply engaged in diagnostic medicine, healthcare leadership, and medical education — scaling lab networks, guiding diagnostic start-ups, and optimizing hospital pathology departments for sustainable growth.

I understand both the science of diagnostics and the business of sustainability. I balance clinical excellence with operational efficiency to create value for organizations and patients alike. My specialization spans ocular pathology, oncology, and advanced diagnostic medicine.

Academically, I have guided over 200 residents and medical professionals, building training programs that produce confident pathologists and laboratory leaders. I am a strong advocate for digital pathology, research integration, and medical education reform.
