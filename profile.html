Professional Profile

Diagnostics Leader · Healthcare Strategist · Board Advisor — 29+ years of experience across pathology, laboratory medicine, healthcare strategy, quality systems, and board governance.

About

Healthcare has undergone remarkable transformation during the course of my career. What began as a journey in pathology gradually evolved into a broader interest in diagnostics leadership, healthcare strategy, quality systems, organizational development, governance, and healthcare innovation. Along the way, I became increasingly fascinated by a simple question: why do some healthcare organizations consistently achieve excellence while others struggle despite having access to similar resources?

The answer, I discovered, lies not in technology alone but in leadership, systems, quality, culture, execution, and the ability to continuously adapt. This belief has shaped my professional journey and continues to influence my work today.

Why Work With Me

My professional journey has provided me with the opportunity to view healthcare from multiple perspectives — clinician, pathologist, laboratory leader, entrepreneur, healthcare strategist, and board member. These experiences allow me to understand not only the scientific dimensions of healthcare, but also the operational, strategic, governance, and growth challenges that organizations face.

Whether supporting diagnostic laboratories, hospital networks, healthcare startups, investors, or emerging healthcare ventures, my focus remains on helping organizations create sustainable value through quality, innovation, leadership, and patient-centered thinking.

As a Board Member & Advisor

The most effective board members and advisors do not simply contribute expertise — they contribute perspective. They help leadership teams balance:

  • Growth with quality
  • Innovation with governance
  • Opportunity with risk
  • Ambition with sustainability

Looking Ahead

Healthcare is entering an era defined by data, precision medicine, genomics, artificial intelligence, and new models of care delivery. I believe diagnostics will play an increasingly central role in shaping that future. My mission is to help healthcare organizations navigate this transformation while remaining grounded in quality, leadership, governance, and patient care.
