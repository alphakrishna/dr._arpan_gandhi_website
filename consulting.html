Diagnostics Consulting

29+ years at the intersection of clinical diagnostics, laboratory operations, and healthcare strategy — now available as structured consulting engagements for organizations that are ready to grow, improve, or transform.

Diagnostic organizations face a specific kind of complexity. Clinical quality, operational efficiency, regulatory compliance, talent, technology, and commercial sustainability must all be managed simultaneously — and the cost of getting any one of them wrong is high. I have navigated these tensions across large diagnostic chains, hospital networks, regional centers, and startup environments for nearly three decades.

My consulting work is hands-on, context-specific, and rooted in what actually works in Indian healthcare. I do not parachute in with generic frameworks. Every engagement begins with a thorough understanding of where you are, where you want to go, and what is genuinely holding you back.

Service Area 01

Laboratory Strategy & Growth

Whether you are entering a new market, restructuring an existing network, or building a long-term growth plan, I help diagnostic organizations make sharper strategic decisions with better clinical and commercial outcomes.

  • Network expansion planning and geographic feasibility studies
  • New laboratory setup — business case, specialization strategy, and location selection
  • Revenue growth frameworks and test portfolio optimization
  • Competitive positioning and market entry strategy for tier 2 and tier 3 cities
  • Diagnostic enterprise restructuring and P&L improvement
Service Area 02

Laboratory Operations & Performance Improvement

Operational excellence in diagnostics is not about cutting costs — it is about delivering consistent quality at speed and scale. I identify where workflows break down, where turnaround time is lost, and where capacity is being left on the table.

  • Operational assessment, workflow mapping, and redesign
  • Turnaround time (TAT) optimization and throughput analysis
  • Cost structure review, benchmarking, and efficiency improvement
  • LIS implementation, digital infrastructure, and reporting alignment
  • Staffing models, shift design, and productivity frameworks
Service Area 03

Quality Systems & Accreditation

I have been a CAP Internal Auditor since 1999 and a NABL quality systems expert for over two decades. Quality in diagnostics is not a checkbox — it is a culture. I help organizations build that culture, and then get the credentials to prove it.

  • NABL accreditation — gap analysis, preparation, mock audits, and post-audit support
  • CAP accreditation and Internal Audit Program design and execution
  • CAPA implementation, root cause analysis frameworks, and corrective action tracking
  • SOP development, documentation systems, and version control
  • Quality culture transformation and staff training programs
Service Area 04

Laboratory Setup & Expansion

Setting up a new diagnostic laboratory involves far more than equipment procurement. From regulatory approvals to staffing, from layout design to referral network development — I have been through this process multiple times, in multiple geographies, at multiple scales.

  • Greenfield lab setup — design, equipment planning, staffing, and licensing
  • Satellite lab and collection centre network development
  • Hospital laboratory establishment within healthcare systems
  • Post-setup performance monitoring, ramp-up support, and quality baseline setting
  • COVID-19 RT-PCR and rapid diagnostics lab setup (proven track record)
Service Area 05

Diagnostic Transformation & Future Readiness

Diagnostics is changing faster than most organizations can keep up. Genomics, AI-assisted pathology, digital reporting, and precision medicine are no longer distant possibilities — they are choices that diagnostic leaders must make today. I help organizations navigate this shift with clarity and confidence.

  • AI and digital pathology adoption roadmap and vendor evaluation
  • Genomics and molecular diagnostics integration planning
  • Digital health partnerships and data strategy for diagnostic enterprises
  • M&A integration — operational, clinical, and cultural alignment across acquired centers
  • Innovation pipeline development and future diagnostics leadership

How I Work

Every diagnostic organization has a unique history, culture, and set of constraints. The most effective consulting is not about applying a template — it is about deeply understanding the specific context and designing solutions that will actually work within it.

My engagements typically move through three phases:

  • Understand — deep assessment of current state, challenges, and opportunities
  • Design — tailored recommendations with clear priorities and implementation paths
  • Support — hands-on guidance through execution, not just a report and an exit
